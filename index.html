About Us

Living Life On Your Own Terms

In the era of modern families, single mothers are still perceived differently, whether with pity, wonder, or derision. Their parental authority is effectively questioned by the absence of a father. We have created a website with a chatbot in order to offer single mothers a support system that is similar to that offered by their families. Our website will offer support for both mother and baby's physical and mental health as well as additional services like skill development, healthier eating options, and much more.

Our Features

The lack of family, monetary support and healthcare for women during pregnancy in our country is still a case to be addressed specially for single mothers as they are still considered a taboo and thus we provide "Motherhood" to the society.
